My projects

01.

RoboMental

Humanoid Robot - 2016

ADVISOR

Ali Balta

RoboMental, which I have developed as open source and produced using 3d printer, helps paralysed people express themselves by the help of EEG system as well as its basic humanoid robotic activities. Patients can command both with verbal statements or mimics and thoughts. I have completed the first phase of the project. Prototype is currently performing its aim experimentally. My studies continue to make its movements more balanced and to use the EEG system more easily.

02.

RoboCar

Security Robot - 2015

ADVISOR

Ali Balta Ali Tüylü

Robotext is a robotic platform that processes autonomously and that you can entrust indoors and that you can remotely control with your mobile. This robot moves where you place it, charges by the help of its panels where it perceives the sun, checks constantly out the environment with its movement, sound, fire, water and gas sensors and shots the negative situations with its camera (theft, open windows and etc.) and sends mails to you. You can connect from wherever you want and you can see and wander simultaneously.

FormuLaz

Electricity Car - 2015

WITH

Ahmet Metin Bıyık

ADVISOR

Ali Balta Kemalettin Gültekin

FormuLaz, a means of transport with electricity. It provides its own power with its solar panels. I study on software and electronics phases of the project. I have added peculiarities such as trip computer, parking sensor and smart key. We are studying on long life battery and lightness.
